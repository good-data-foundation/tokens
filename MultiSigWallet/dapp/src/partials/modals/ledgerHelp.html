Unlock your Ledger wallet

In order to use the multisig with your Ledger wallet you need to:

  • Connect your Ledger wallet to your USB port
  • Enter your Ledger wallet pin code
  • Update ledger firmware if version < 1.2
  • Install the Ethereum app on your Ledger wallet
  • Enable Browser support and contract data on settings
  • Disable Browser support and enable contract data on settings
  • Allow the multisig DApp to access your accounts on the Ledger wallet

Your Ledger is now connected to Multisig.
